The interactive short film "Manner of Death (Dear. J)" is an investigative genre that tells the story of "J," a 28-year-old police officer, who faces the unexpected death of his lover, "Dear," a 25-year-old graphic designer. The interactive film simulates the experience of being J, as he investigates his lover's death to determine the manner of death and cause of death. Viewers (a.k.a Viewsers) can choose different investigative approaches by clicking buttons on the website. At the end, the system prompts the user to answer questions about the circumstances and reasons for his lover's death. Finally, at the end of the short film, a movie ticket will appear, which viewers can save and share on social media.
